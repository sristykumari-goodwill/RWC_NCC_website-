    NCC

    The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a youth organization in India that trains school and college students to develop discipline, leadership, and patriotism. It is run by the Ministry of Defence and gives basic military training to young students. NCC is open to both boys and girls and has three wings:

  • Army Wing
  • Navy Wing
  • Air Wing
    • Cadets in NCC take part in activities like drill, camps, weapon training, social service, and adventure programs. These activities help them become confident, responsible, and physically fit. NCC also offers certificates (A, B, and C) which are very helpful for careers in defence services, police, and government jobs.
  • 👉 In simple words:
  • NCC is a platform that turns students into disciplined and confident future leaders who are ready to serve the nation.

    • Aim

    Unity and Dicipline

  • To develop qualities of character, courage, comradeship, discipline, leadership, secular outlook, spirit of adventure and sportsmanship and the ideals of selfless service among the youth to make them useful citizen.

  • To create a human resource of organized trained and motivated youth to provide leadership in all walks of life including the Armed Forces and be always available for the service of the nation

    • Oath

  • "I do hereby solemnly promise that I will serve my motherland most truly and loyally and that, I will abide by the rules and regulations of the National Cadet Crops. Further under the command and control of my commanding officer I will participate in every camp most sincerely and wholeheartedly".

    • Pledge

  • We the cadet of the national cadet corps, do solemnly pledge that we shall always uphold the unity of India. We resolve to be disciplined and responsible citizen of our nation. We shall undertake positive community service in the spirit of selflessness and concern for our fellow beings.

    • Objectives of NCC

  • - To fulfill the requirements expected in the latest socio-economic scenario in India.
  • - To develop character, discipline, comradeship, secular outlook, ideals of selfless services and adventure spirits amongst large numbers of young citizens.
  • - To create a pool of trained, organized and motivated youth with various leadership qualities in every walk of their life and serve the Nation irrespective of career they choose.
  • - To provide a sound environment conducive towards motivating large numbers of young people of India to join armed forces.

    • wings in NCC

    The NCC comprises three main wings—Army, Navy, and Air—training youth in discipline, leadership, and adventure under the motto "Unity and Discipline." Each wing offers specialized training and certificates (A, B, C) that provide direct entry (SSB) opportunities to the armed forces.

    NCC Wings:

  • Army Wing:

    Focuses on drills, weapon training, adventure training, and disaster relief.

  • Naval Wing:

    Provides training in seamanship, navigation, communication, and sailing.

  • Air Wing:

    Specializes in aerodynamics, flight theory, and micro-light flying opportunities, ideal for aviation enthusiasts.
