Incentives to Cadets in NCC

🎖️ Career Benefits

  • Direct entry in Armed Forces (Special Entry for 'C' Certificate)
  • Bonus marks in government jobs
  • Preference in Police & Paramilitary forces
  • Helps in SSB interview preparation

🎓 Educational Benefits

  • Scholarships for deserving cadets
  • Preference in college admissions
  • Participation in RDC & National Camps
  • Youth Exchange Program (foreign visits)

🏕️ Training & Exposure

  • Free military-style training
  • Adventure activities (trekking, firing, camps)
  • Leadership & discipline development
  • Real defense environment exposure

🌟 Personality Development

  • Improves confidence & communication
  • Develops leadership qualities
  • Encourages social service
  • Builds patriotism

🏆 Recognition & Awards

  • A, B, C Certificates
  • Best Cadet Awards
  • National level recognition
  • Opportunities to represent state/college
