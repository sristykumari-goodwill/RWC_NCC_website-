About us

NCC

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) unit of Ranchi Women’s College, Ranchi, is a proud and disciplined unit dedicated to shaping young women into confident, responsible, and nation-loving citizens. Our unit is affiliated with the 3 Jharkhand Girls Battalion NCC, Hotwar, Ranchi, and function under the Army Wing. At Ranchi Women’s College NCC, we aim to instill the core values of unity, discipline, leadership, and selfless service among our cadets. Through regular training, drills, camps, and social service activities, cadets develop physical fitness, mental strength, and a strong sense of responsibility towards society and the nation. Our cadets actively participate in various NCC camps such as Annual Training Camps (ATC), Combined Annual Training Camps (CATC), and national-level camps including Republic Day Camp (RDC) and Thal Sainik Camp (TSC). These experiences help them gain exposure, confidence, and leadership skills at both state and national levels. The unit also takes part in community development programs like cleanliness drives, awareness rallies, blood donation camps, and disaster management activities, contributing positively to society.
Joining NCC at Ranchi Women’s College not only enhances personality development but also opens doors to various career opportunities in defense services and other prestigious fields. Our mission is to empower young women to become future leaders with courage, commitment, and a spirit of patriotism.

College

  • About Ranchi Women's College Established in 1949, Ranchi Women’s College is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions for women’s education in Jharkhand.
  • The college is affiliated with Ranchi University, ensuring quality academic standards and recognized degrees.
  • It offers a wide range of Undergraduate and Postgraduate courses in Arts, Science, and Commerce streams.
  • The institution is dedicated to empowering women through education, helping students become confident, independent, and responsible citizens.
  • The college has a well-developed campus with classrooms, laboratories, a library, and other essential facilities for academic growth.
  • It encourages students to participate in co-curricular and extracurricular activities, including NCC, NSS, cultural programs, and sports.
  • Ranchi Women’s College is known for promoting discipline, leadership, and social responsibility among students.
  • The college has produced many successful alumni who have excelled in various professional fields.
  • It plays a vital role in the educational and social development of Jharkhand, especially in promoting higher education for women.
